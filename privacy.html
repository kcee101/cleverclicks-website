Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you express an interest in obtaining information about us or our services. This includes names, email addresses, phone numbers, and any other details you provide in contact forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to respond to your inquiries, provide you with the services you have requested, and send you marketing and promotional communications (if you have opted in). We do not share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

3. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, please remember that we cannot guarantee that the internet itself is 100% secure.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you and to ask for that data to be corrected or deleted. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us at support@cleverclicksconcept.com.
